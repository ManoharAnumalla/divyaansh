Home Services

General Medicine

Comprehensive care for adults, focusing on the diagnosis and treatment of various non-surgical conditions, fevers, and chronic illnesses.

Gynaecology & Infertility

Expert care in women's health, from adolescence to menopause, including high-risk pregnancies and advanced infertility treatments.

Orthopedic

Specialized diagnosis and treatment of bone, joint, ligament, tendon injuries, trauma care, and precision joint replacements.

ENT

State-of-the-art medical and surgical care for patients with diseases and disorders of the ear, nose, throat, and related structures.

Dermatology

Diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail problems, along with cosmetic dermatology services for enhanced well-being.

Neurology

Advanced care for disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles using the latest diagnostic tools.

URO & Nephrology

Comprehensive care for urinary tract conditions and kidney diseases, including dialysis and minimally invasive surgeries.

Cardiology

World-class heart care offering diagnosis, medical management, and interventional procedures for various cardiovascular conditions.

Management of digestive system disorders, equipped with advanced endoscopy and colonoscopy facilities.

General Surgery

A wide range of surgical procedures, from routine appendectomies to complex abdominal surgeries, with options for laparoscopy.

Pediatrics

Dedicated medical care for infants, children, and adolescents, providing routine check-ups, vaccinations, and specialized pediatric treatment.
